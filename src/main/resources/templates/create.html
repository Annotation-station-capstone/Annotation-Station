TURN YOUR TYPED NOTES INTO
TIMESTAMPED BOOKMARKS FOR VIDEOS

Enter a URL of a video that you wish to take notes about, type in the text field below and press enter. Each entry will become part of a list of links that will send you to the moment in the video it was created. Want to enter more information on subsequent re-watches? Any entry made after the first viewing will be logged in the original list and ordered to fit chronologically with the videos time. When you are finished save the information for later use or send it to a friend. Enjoy!
